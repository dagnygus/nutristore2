2.5. Nutristore.com reserves the right to refuse to execute orders that raise doubts, in particular when the history of cooperation undermines the credibility and reliability of the customer.

3. TRANSPORT, PAYMENTS AND COMPLETION DATES

3.1. The ordered goods are delivered to the address indicated in the order form via Poczta Polska (priority parcel) or a courier company, and in the case of foreign shipments - by Poczta Polska (registered priority).

3.2. The order fulfillment time is from 2 hours to 10 business days. If the execution time turns out to be longer than the one specified on our website, we contact the customer to inform about the delay and confirm that the order will be processed further.

3.3. In the case of payment by bank transfer, the above deadline is additionally extended by the time of the transfer, and in the case of card payments, by the time of payment authorization by an external payment system.

3.4. Nutristore.com is not directly responsible for delays in delivery caused by the failure by courier companies to comply with the transport and postal service contracts. However, we try to intervene in such situations so that the customer receives the package as soon as possible.

3.5. All information about the status of the contract is provided by e-mail at contact@nutristore.com.

3.6. The following forms of payment are possible to be chosen by the Customer:

a) On delivery - payment in cash to the courier, postman or at the post office upon receipt of the goods or at the cash desk of the personal collection point. Nutristore.com reserves the right to additional telephone verification of this type of orders in certain situations.

b) By bank transfer - after booking the ordered goods, an e-mail with the bank account number is sent. As soon as the payment is credited to our bank account, the order is transferred to the warehouse to be prepared for shipment.

c) Electronic payments. For security reasons, each electronic payment transaction is verified by the operator, the verification date does not depend on the store. The order is processed immediately after the payment is confirmed.

3.7. The ordering party covers the shipping cost. The shipping cost is calculated by the system automatically for each order based on the products added to the cart and displayed to the customer before placing it.

3.8. Choosing cash on delivery as a form of delivery with billing means that the customer is aware of the preparation of an amount equal to the value of the order shown in the confirmation. The customer is obliged to prepare the exact amount to be paid.

4.1. Pursuant to the Act of March 2, 2000 on the protection of certain consumer rights and liability for damage caused by a dangerous product (Journal of Laws No. 22, item 271), Art. 2 point 2, within ten days of receiving the goods ordered by mail order, the consumer has the right to return it as part of withdrawal from the contract, in accordance with the rules resulting from the provisions on concluding distance contracts. The law indicated in this point applies to consumer sales, so it applies only to sales to a natural person who makes a purchase for purposes not related to professional or business activity. Goods returned in this mode will be accepted only if they are sent back in their original, undamaged packaging, with all its contents, and the product itself does not bear any traces of use (i.e. has not been opened, tried on, or damaged). If you wish to exercise the right to withdraw from the contract, the returned goods should be sent back at your own expense, after informing the Store in advance. The return shipment must be accompanied by a written declaration of withdrawal from the contract. The Nutristore.com store does not refund the costs of delivery and return of the ordered products. We do not accept "cash on delivery" shipments.

4.2. When choosing the "cash on delivery" payment option, if the package has been sent (the status has been changed to "shipped"), in the event of resignation, the customer is obliged to cover the double value of shipping costs to the Nutristore.com Store account (shipping + return) within 7 days of receiving the return under the pain of initiating recovery proceedings. In the case of returning paid parcels (e.g. when the courier did not find the customer several times), Nutristore.com offers the possibility of re-sending the parcel after covering the shipping costs.

4.3. You should ALWAYS check the contents of the package at the courier, even if the package does not look damaged. Complaints about mechanical damage caused during transport and the lack of goods will be considered faster and more efficiently in the situation of receipt / inspection of the goods in the presence of the courier. The courier is obliged to write a report in the event of irregularities or damage by the customer.

4.4. The goods under complaint, together with a letter specifying the type of non-compliance and the expectations regarding the manner of fulfilling our obligations, should be sent to the operator of the Nutristore.com Store.

4.5. In case of a mistake on our part, it is possible to replace the product. The product cannot be unsealed. The parcel should be sent by Poczta Polska (only and exclusively) within its scope to the sender's address (please contact us in advance before shipping).
